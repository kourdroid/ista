Frequently Asked Question

Founded in 1974, OFPPT is a public training institution. It offers vocational training free of charge. The training is based on a demand-driven strategy meeting the qualification needs and requirements of the labor market. OFPPT has a large network of training centers all over Morocco. These centers provide acknowledged competencies at the service of business companies.

Program durations vary depending on the field of study. You can find detailed information about the duration of each program on the respective program pages.

To apply for a program, please visit our Application Page and follow the instructions provided.

You can reach out to our admissions team by Contacting them in the Contact page
